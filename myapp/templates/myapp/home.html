Welcome to the Home Page

The purpose of this site is to gather data on how good humans are at identifying the contents of camouflaged images in relation to how good various AI models are at the same task.

When you click start, you will be shown a series of images containing either empty sea floor or camouflaged animals. You will be asked to identify if an animal is present in the image. If you think an animal is present, you will also be asked which kind of animal you think it is.

All data recorded is anonymous.
